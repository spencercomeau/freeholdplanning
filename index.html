Our Specialty

Retirement income and tax planning is what we do best.

Most Canadians accumulate savings over decades, but nobody teaches them how to draw it down. That's where we come in. We build detailed, year-by-year plans that answer the questions that keep you up at night.

Retirement Income & Tax Planning

This is the core of what we do. We build year-by-year projections showing exactly how your RRSP, TFSA, RRIF, CPP, OAS, and workplace pension work together to produce retirement income, and how to draw down in the most tax-efficient order possible. Our plans cover CPP and OAS timing analysis, RRSP meltdown strategies, RRIF conversion timing, OAS clawback avoidance, pension income splitting, and tax bracket management across your entire retirement. You get a written plan with clear numbers, not a vague conversation.

CPP & OAS Optimization

When to take CPP isn't a simple question. We model the break-even scenarios, consider your health, other income sources, and tax bracket to find the timing that puts the most money in your pocket.

Tax-Optimized Decumulation

The order you draw from your accounts matters more than most people realize. RRSP meltdown strategies, TFSA optimization, and pension income splitting are all part of a plan that minimizes what goes to CRA.

About

Built on experience, driven by independence.

Freehold Financial Planning specializes in retirement income and tax planning. We help Canadians figure out how to turn decades of savings into reliable, tax-efficient income that lasts. That's our core focus, and it's where we do our best work.

Our team brings years of experience from inside Canada's banking system. We saw firsthand how the advisory model works: advisors are measured on assets gathered and products sold, not on the quality of advice delivered. Good people, doing their best within a system that isn't always designed to put the client first.

Freehold is different by design. We have no inventory to move, no sales targets to hit, and no employer whose products we're expected to recommend. When we recommend a course of action, it's because we believe it's right for you.

Beyond retirement planning, we also offer comprehensive financial plans, goal-based planning, second opinions, and estate and insurance reviews. But retirement income and the tax decisions around it are at the heart of everything we do.
